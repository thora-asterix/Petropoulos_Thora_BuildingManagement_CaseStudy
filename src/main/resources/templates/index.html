How to start the app

×

  1. Create your apartments and choose apartment numbers in the 1000s according to floor.
  2. Next created your services. The services are companies and people that will serve your building for daily tasks or issues with the apartments.
  3. At this point you can add tasks to your interface. Tasks depend on services so they can only been created when you already have at least one service in your application.
  4. Tenants can be add the tenants can be added after the apartements are available in your application.
  5. Issues can be added after the services and the apartments are added to your application.
